About Us

Built on Precision and Trust

Crescent Prestige Autohaus is a specialist European automotive workshop located in the heart of Homebush, serving drivers across the Inner West, the Parramatta Road corridor, and greater Sydney. We were established to offer something the area was missing: a workshop that brings genuine European expertise together with the honest, straightforward service of a trusted local mechanic.

Our focus has always been the European marques — BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, Volkswagen, Volvo, Alfa Romeo, and more. These vehicles are engineered to a standard that rewards specialist knowledge, and our team has invested the time to understand them deeply. We work with the right diagnostic tools, the right parts, and the right approach for vehicles that demand the best.

At the same time, Crescent Prestige Autohaus has always been a workshop for everyone. Whether you drive a prestige European or a dependable everyday car, you’ll receive the same level of care, the same honest communication, and the same commitment to getting it right. Our 5.0 Google rating reflects that commitment — and the trust our customers have placed in us.
